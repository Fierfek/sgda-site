UCI VGDC - INTRO TO SHADERS IN UNITY
DESCRIPTION

This workshop serves as an introduction to writing custom vertex-fragment shaders in Unity. It will focus on effects that work in 2D without lighting, such as dissolve effects and texture distortion.

*Bringing a laptop with Unity installed is recommended.

CSUF VGDC - Game Marketing
DESCRIPTION

So you've finished making your game. Now what?

If you plan on sharing your project to the world, then be prepared to find out why game marketing is so important. The workshop will cover ways to market (from an student/indie/low budget perspective), tips and considerations, and YES - be prepared to stand up and talk!
