STUDENT GAMES FESTIVAL | 2020 GAMES SHOWCASE
SGDA Student Games Showcase
DESCRIPTION

One of the highlights of the Student Games Festival is the chance to celebrate and showcase our hard work! Join members from various clubs from SGDA and students from members schools of IEEE GameSIG as we demo our games to the public.

SUBMISSION

If you would like to showcase your game, please fill out the Student Showcase Form. After submitting the form, please submit the build of your game with all required files to this itch.io by 11:59 PM, April 25th. Your submission will be reviewed and we will reach out to you in order to provide short interviews to ensure that your presentation can be the best it can be prior to the event.
