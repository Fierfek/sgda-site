Andrew Robinson - "How Story Shapes the Gaming Experience"
(SENIOR WRITER - BLIZZARD ENTERAINMENT)
BIO

Having written dozens of hours of animation for over thirty television series, Andrew is now writing animation, comics, short fiction and other content for Blizzard Entertainment, supplementing and informing the games of WoW, StarCraft II, Heroes of the Storm, Diablo, Hearthstone and Overwatch.

DESCRIPTION

Andrew will talk about how and why he came to Blizzard, about writing for the Story and Franchise Development department, and how storytelling via cinematics and other media helps to build bigger and more complete worlds for players to view, engage, and emotionally invest in.

Anthony Davis - "Life in the game industry"
(Senior Gameplay Programmer - Obsidian Entertainment)
BIO

In Anthony's 10+ years at Obsidian Entertainment, he has worked on a variety of titles including Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II, Neverwinter Nights 2, Alpha Protocol, Dungeon Siege III, South Park: The Stick of Truth, and Armored Warfare.

DESCRIPTION

Join Anthony as he shares his experiences working in the game industry including memorable people, funny anecdotes and horror stories, as well as his personal journey into the game industry and tips for those wanting to get into it.
